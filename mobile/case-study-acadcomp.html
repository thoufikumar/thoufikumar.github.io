Student & Campus Companion App

Reducing uncertainty and waiting in academic meetings.

  • UX / UI Designer
  • Mobile App
  • Nov 2024
  • UX Case Study

Overview

Academic Companion is a campus utility platform that enables students, teachers, and staff to manage attendance, meals, and transactions seamlessly.

The Problem

Campus operations struggled due to manual attendance tracking and inefficient meal transactions.

Why it matters?

  • Wasted time – Manual attendance verification
  • Delayed transactions – Lack of balance visibility
  • Purchase uncertainty – No balance visibility

Who it impacts?

  • Students buying meals – Unclear balance tracking
  • Store staff processing payments – Slow manual verification

Existing Approaches

  • Manual Attendance
  • Unstructured Meal Access
  • Limited Balance Visibility
  • Operational Delays
  • Lack of Real-time Feedback

Ideation & Process

How I approached it?

  • Observed real student behavior
  • Reviewed attendance validation process
  • Focused on reducing interaction uncertainty

Key decisions Made

  • Enabled context switching
  • Made scanner the primary interface
  • Added instant confirmations

Design Execution

Overall app is summarized into 3 steps:

Attendance QR System

A student can

  • View QR – Show to faculty
  • QR updates every few seconds
  • Track scans – See recent attendance
  • Refresh manually
Meal QR system

A faculty can

  • Show QR – Present code to staff
  • QR updates every few seconds
  • View today's allowance usage
Balance Allowance

A student can

  • Check remaining program allowance
  • Understand daily transaction deductions
  • See snack or meal payments

Features for Faculty & Store staff

Helps faculty and store staff scan attendance and track transactions efficiently.

Usability testing & validation

Who tested it?

Tested with a small group of students and faculty.

What I observed?

Users lacked clarity on request status and faculty responses.

What changed?

Added clear status updates and confirmation feedback.

Final Outcome

  • Reduced delays in attendance validation
  • Improved clarity in meal transactions
  • Faster operational workflow for staff
