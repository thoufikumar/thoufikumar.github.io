Smartmeet

Student-Faculty Appointment Scheduling Application

Reducing uncertainty and waiting in academic meetings.

  • UI/UX Designer
  • MOBILE APP
  • Nov 2024
  • UX Case Study

Overview

SmartMeet helps students request and manage faculty meetings without uncertainty or delays.

The Problem

Students struggled to meet faculty due to unclear availability and unstructured communication.

Why it matters?

  • Wasted time - Unclear faculty availability
  • Missed meetings - Lack of confirmation flow
  • Anxiety around follow-ups - No status visibility

Who it impacts?

  • Students seeking guidance - Unstructured meeting requests
  • Faculty managing requests - Overlapping, unmanaged schedules

Existing Approaches

  • Physical faculty visits
  • WhatsApp or email requests
  • Phone call scheduling
  • Informal calendar slots

Ideation & Process

How I approached it ?

  • Focused on reducing decision friction
  • Studied existing scheduling patterns
  • Observed real student behavior

Key decisions Made

  • Prioritized status visibility
  • Made faculty the final confirms
  • Removed fixed-time requirement

Design Execution

Overall app is summarized into 3 steps:

New meeting request

A student can

  • Search faculty - Apply filters
  • Choose a preset reason for the meet
  • See Preview Card
  • Submit Request
Meet OR System

A student can

  • View Urgent, Pending, New requests
  • View today's meetings
  • Can set time & venue for the meet
Student gets notified

A student gets the

  • Meetings details in life view
  • Gets timely reminders
  • Can also reschedule the meet

Usability testing & validation

Who tested it?

Tested with a small group of college students and faculty members.

What i observed?

Early tests revealed confusion around request status and faculty responses.

What changed?

Added clear status indicators and confirmation feedback.

Final Outcome

  • Reduced unnecessary in-person visits
  • Improved clarity around meeting status
  • Positive feedback from test users
