SelfSpace

Mental Wellness App

What is SelfSpace?

SelfSpace is a mental wellness app for emotional reflection, guided exercises, and mindful support.

What Techstacks are used?

Built using Flutter, Dart, Firebase Authentication, and SharedPreferences.

Why it matters?

Many people struggle to understand and manage their emotions without structured support.

Future Enhancements ?

Future enhancements include AI-powered emotional insights and a redesigned V2 experience.
